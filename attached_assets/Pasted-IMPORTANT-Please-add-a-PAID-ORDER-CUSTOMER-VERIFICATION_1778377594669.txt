==================================================
PAID ORDER VERIFICATION SYSTEM
==============================

For ALL prepaid / paid orders:

Before marking order as delivered, rider MUST verify customer.

==================================================
REQUIRED VERIFICATION OPTIONS
=============================

Rider App should allow:

✅ customer photo capture
✅ CNIC / ID card photo
✅ house/front door photo
✅ parcel handover proof
✅ optional customer signature

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT
==============

Rider should NOT be able to complete prepaid order UNTIL at least one verification proof is uploaded.

==================================================
DELIVERY VERIFICATION FLOW
==========================

STEP 1
Rider reaches customer.

STEP 2
App opens verification screen automatically.

STEP 3
Rider captures:

✅ customer image
OR
✅ ID card image
OR
✅ house/front image

STEP 4
Image uploads instantly to server/database.

STEP 5
Order marked:
✅ VERIFIED & DELIVERED
