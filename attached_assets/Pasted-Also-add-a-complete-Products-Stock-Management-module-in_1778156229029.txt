# KG/Gram Logic (IMPORTANT)

Products should NOT be duplicated.

Wrong:
❌ Cashew 250g
❌ Cashew 500g

Correct:
✅ Cashew Nuts

Then cashier selects weight dynamically:

* 250g
* 500g
* 1KG

Stock auto deducts correctly.
