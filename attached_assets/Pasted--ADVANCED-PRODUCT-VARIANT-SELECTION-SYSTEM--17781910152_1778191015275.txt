========================
ADVANCED PRODUCT VARIANT SELECTION SYSTEM
=========================================

The AI ecommerce chat system must properly support product variants and conversational product selection.

Currently the chatbot is not handling variants professionally.

Example:

Customer searches:
✅ Almonds
✅ Cashews
✅ Pista
✅ Akhrot

But products contain multiple variants like:

* 250g
* 500g
* 1kg
* 2kg

The chatbot must intelligently guide the customer through variant selection instead of directly placing incomplete orders.
