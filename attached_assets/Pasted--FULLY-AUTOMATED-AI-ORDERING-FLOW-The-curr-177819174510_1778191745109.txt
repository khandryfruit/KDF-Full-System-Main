========================
FULLY AUTOMATED AI ORDERING FLOW
================================

The current system still depends too much on manual clicking.

That is NOT the goal.

Main goal is:
✅ Customers who cannot type properly
✅ Customers who do not understand ecommerce
✅ Customers who are not tech-savvy

should STILL be able to place orders easily through AI conversation.

Need fully conversational AI ordering system.

========================
NATURAL HUMAN-LIKE AI FLOW
==========================

The AI should behave like real sales staff.

Example:

Customer:
“Mujhe badam order karne hain”

AI:
“Ji sir, aapko Almonds kaunsa weight chahiye?”

✅ 250g
✅ 500g
✅ 1kg

Customer:
“1 kilo”

AI should automatically:
✅ Detect selected variant
✅ Add correct variant to order form
✅ Update pricing automatically
✅ Continue conversation naturally

WITHOUT forcing customer to manually fill everything.

========================
AUTO ORDER BUILDING
===================

The system should intelligently build order automatically during conversation.

AI should:
✅ Detect products
✅ Detect quantities
✅ Detect variants
✅ Detect address
✅ Detect payment method
✅ Build checkout automatically

Example:

Customer:
“Mujhe 1 kilo badam aur 500 gram pista chahiye”

AI automatically:
✅ Adds Almonds 1kg
✅ Adds Pista 500g
✅ Calculates total
✅ Opens confirmation flow

========================
SMART CONVERSATIONAL CHECKOUT
=============================

AI should ask only missing details naturally.

Example:

AI:
“Sir delivery kis city mein chahiye?”

Customer:
“Lahore Johar Town”

AI:
✅ Auto-detects city
✅ Auto-detects area
✅ Syncs Google Maps location
✅ Updates order automatically

Then AI asks:
“Cash on delivery ya online payment?”
